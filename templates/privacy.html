Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access or disclosure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers do not have access to your expense data beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on the site. We will respond to all reasonable requests in a timely manner.
